Donations & Contributions:

It's important to me that making these websites be a donation-based thing. I want to make and maintain these websites for you guys because I respect what you do as artists and I want to support you and your work. With that said many of you have already been super generous in offering to pay me for building your sites, so to honor that I've set up a Paypal funding page for the next expanded version of this site.

The next version of artpages will hopefully end up being a kind of donation-based Squarespace clone which would allow you guys to easily build and update your websites yourselves from home. Any money you pitch in here will go directly towards helping to quickly develop and launch the best possible version of that next evolution of the site.

Thanks everybody for all the support so far on this!
