Donations & Contributions:

As stated in the Information section linked above, participating artists are asked to pay whatever they can whenever they can to support the project, in the hopes of maintaining the viability of the project and the development of more features that will streamline the process of building and maintaing the websites of all current and future participating artists.

Thank you in advance! ~ Tom


Helping to Fund the Project...

For the time being donations can be made through PayPal, which takes $0.30 out of every contribution plus an additional 2.9% of the total contribution amount.

If you plan to donate now or in the near future, thank you so much for supporting this project and it's community of participating artists!

Helping to Develop the Project...

The easiest thing you can do to help develop the project is to give feedback! If you find any bugs or points of confusion etc. just drop an email to the address below with the subject "AP Feedback" and be as specific as you can.

If you happen to be an artist capable in basic web-development or an art-sympathetic web-developer with too much time on your hands you might be in a position to help develop new templates and functions for this project. If you think you might be interested in helping please send an email outlining your interest-level and pertinant experience to the address below with the subject "AP Contribute."
