About this Site:

Right now this site is just a hub for instructions and web templates that I've put together for a small group of family and friends. It exists to help you get me what I need so I can put together / update websites for you guys quickly and professionally. In the near future, I'm hoping to expand the site into a kind of donation-based Squarespace clone where you guys would be able to update your websites yourselves at home and maybe from your phones as well.

If you're all ready for me to build a site for you, you can start by picking out a Website Template from the linked section above and then follow the Instructions also linked above. And if for any reason you feel inspired to contribute to help make the next version of the site happen (or you're just super happy with your website) you can always check out the Chip In section too!
